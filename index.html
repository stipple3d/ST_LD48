RANDOM DEEP

Ludum Dare 48 Entry by Stipple3D

Instructions

Randomness has decended on the rooms of this Ship. The further you venture from your home room (0,0 coords), the more the force will move you.

Each room has an emmitter which points the way to each of the five containement elements.

When collected, these elements together will bring the randomness to its knees and return the ship to normal.

Move about the rooms with WASD keys.

Press SPACE BAR to continue back to room view from the transition screens.
